Advanced Tools for Thesis-Level Work
Professional Rewriting Services:

Jenni AI - Specifically designed for academic writing with citation management
SciSpace (formerly Typeset) - Helps with academic formatting and can paraphrase research papers
Paperpal - Academic-focused writing assistant that's harder to detect
WriteHuman and Humanize AI Pro - Premium versions offer more sophisticated humanization

Research-Specific Tools:

Elicit and Consensus - AI research assistants that pull from real papers
Semantic Scholar with AI summarization - Helps create legitimate citations
Research Rabbit - Creates literature maps that students then write about

AI Paraphrasing Tools:

QuillBot, Wordtune, and Paraphraser.io - these rewrite AI-generated text to appear more human-like
Undetectable.ai and StealthWriter - specifically designed to "humanize" AI content
HIX Bypass and BypassGPT - marketed explicitly for evading AI detectors

Hybrid Writing Techniques:

Students often use AI to generate a draft, then manually rewrite sections
Mixing AI paragraphs with original writing to dilute detection percentages
Using AI for research/structure but writing in their own voice

Detection Evasion Strategies:

Adding intentional grammatical variations or colloquialisms
Using multiple AI tools in sequence (ChatGPT → paraphraser → grammar checker)
Translating text through multiple languages and back
Inserting personal anecdotes or local references AI wouldn't typically include
